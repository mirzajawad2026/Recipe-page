Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. You can add a splash of milk or water for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the pan is hot, pour in the egg mixture. Gently push the edges of the omelette to allow uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  4. Add fillings(Optional): When the eggs begin to set, add your desired fillings to one side of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette in half and serve it on a plate with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
